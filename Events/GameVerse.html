GAMEVERSE

february,2026

GameVerse is the only non-technical event at RoboYudh 2026. It features a collection of exciting and entertaining non-technical games designed to engage participants while ensuring maximum fun. Each event also offers attractive prize money. What more could a participant ask for than enjoying fun-filled games and winning rewards? Participants are guaranteed an unforgettable and enjoyable experience.

  • All participants will receive a Certificate of Participation from Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology.
  • A prize pool of INR 1,60,000 will be awarded to the winning teams.

GameVerse Info

Game 1

BOUNCE TO BRAIN

  • Each team must consist of 4 participants.
  • A start point and end point are marked for the first challenge, which will be attempted by Participant 1.
  • A problem-solving task is placed at the end point for Participant 2.
  • A movement-based coordination layout with specific placement instructions is prepared for Participant 3.
  • The fourth participant will complete a designated run through the activity area three times, and the total time taken will be recorded.

GAME 2

FLIP TAC TOE

  • The game is played between two teams, with 2 players in each team.
  • Both teams will attempt the challenge simultaneously.
  • If a team completes the attempt successfully, one member places a mark on the grid.
  • Teams must alternate roles between attempting and placing after each round.
  • The first team to complete the required pattern on the grid wins the game.

GAME 3

BOTTLE WALKER

  • The game is played between two teams, with 2 players in each team.
  • Both teams will attempt the challenge simultaneously.
  • If a team completes the attempt successfully, the object moves one step forward.
  • The team that reaches the final position first is declared the winner.

GAME 4

PICTURE PLOT

  • The game is played in teams of 2 members.
  • The game consists of two rounds.
  • Teams must create a connected story using the given visuals.
  • Each story must include a title and a clear message.
  • Teams are judged based on creativity and clarity, and the best teams win.

GAME 5

SPELL bEE

  • The game is played in teams of 2 members.
  • Words will be given orally by the host.
  • Teams are allowed to ask for one clarification per word.
  • Correct spellings earn points; incorrect spellings lose points.
  • The team with the highest score at the end wins.
